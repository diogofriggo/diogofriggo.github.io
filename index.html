2010-2012

Started degree in Computer Science

Ever more fascinated with programming I decided to step up my game and got into university. I struggled in some classes mostly because I couldn't in any way get interested in them
On the other hand I loved and excelled at many STEM subjects like:

  • Probability theory
  • Linear algebra
  • Calculus (got invited to mentor other students)

There were also a lot of other classes that were not exactly my cup of tea but were worth learning like Computer Architecture, Logic Circuits, Ethics and Philosophy

At the end of two years I reflected upon the choices I had made so far and the path that they have led me to
Even though I had no regrets, after those STEM classes I felt that I needed to learn more about applied mathematics, physics in particular and so I left Computer Stience for good

Nevertheless, since programming has been an integral part of my life ever since, I frequently ended up studying the field since then and to this day
